Simple English sentences for basic language learning.

# Greetings and introductions
Hello! My name is Alex. Nice to meet you.
Good morning. How are you today?
Good evening. Have a nice night.

# Everyday phrases
Thank you very much. You're welcome.
Please, could you help me? I need some assistance.
I'm sorry. I didn't understand.
Excuse me, where is the restroom?

# Common questions and answers
What is your name? My name is Sam.
How old are you? I am twenty five years old.
Where do you live? I live in Berlin.
What do you do? I work as a teacher.

# Simple instructions
Open the door. Close the window.
Turn on the light. Turn off the computer.

# Numbers and counting
One, two, three, four, five.
Ten, twenty, thirty, forty, fifty.
Hundred, thousand, million.

# Colors and objects
Red apple, blue sky, green grass.
The cat is on the table.
The book is on the shelf.

# Time expressions
Today, tomorrow, yesterday.
Morning, afternoon, night.
It is five o'clock.

# Basic adjectives
big, small, tall, short, fast, slow.
happy, sad, angry, excited.

# Simple dialogues
A: Hi, how are you?
B: I'm fine, thank you. And you?
A: I'm good, thanks.

These sentences cover fundamental English vocabulary and structures that are easy for a language model to learn and generate coherent, simple responses.
